Elephants

Elephants are quite large animals. They have four legs which resemble large pillars. They have two ears which are like big fans. Elephants have a special body part which is their trunk.

Elephant Babies

Did you know that baby elephants suck their their trunks? These tiny giants don't just do this to soothe themselves, though. It also helps them strengthen their trunk muscles and gain better control over the appendage.
